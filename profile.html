BIO

Anya Naumovic is a multi disciplinary artist mainly focusing on her mediums within the realm of painting and sculpture. Although her work usually analyses meat and the significance of the commodity within society, Naumovic uses strong binary oppositions within her work, the grotesque and the beautiful. which infiltrates it's way into the differing subjects that appear in her work pulling from both trauma and love. These oppositions evoking strong emotional, perhaps self-contained conflicts.
